Consider Reaching Out

If you have experienced any of the warning signs above, consider reaching out to a family member, close friend, teacher, coach, faith leader, or mental health professional.

Does reaching out seem too scary right now? You could try spending some time with our chatbot, journaling about what you're experiencing, or a guided mindfulness meditation.
